About us

Varastohessut avoin yhtiö is a family business founded in 2012. Our expertise is to deliver goods from a warehouse to customers with ease. We do warehousing and international logistics with a help of various partners e.g UPS, DHL, FedEx, Finland Post, GSL.

 

If you are receiving a shipment from us and have something to ask or need help in customs clearance, please contacts us posti@varastohessut.fi
